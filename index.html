Zebras are Awesome

Zebras are single-hoofed animals that are native to Africa. Sign up now and use this website to learn more about Zebras.

Did you know?
Zebras' stripes help confuse predators by creating optical illusions
Zebras are highly social animals that live in large groups
Each Zebra has unique stripe patterns just like human fingerprints
Zebras have excellent hearing, eyesight and powerful legs that help outrun predators
